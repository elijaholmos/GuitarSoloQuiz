Tutorial

Each quiz contains a certain number of questions that will test your knowledge of guitar solos.
At the start of each question, the guitar solo of a song will be played.
After the solo has finished playing, you will be given a selction of song titles.
Select the title of the song that features the guitar solo and you will earn a point.
You will have the option to replay the guitar solo before you select your answer.
